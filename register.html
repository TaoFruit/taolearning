Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to your Education Desires.

How much does it cost?

We service hundreds of learning platforms, so it is best to look at the pricing directly on the service you are seeking. Each program has different rates and various services offered.

Where is the software?

We utilize Office 365. After you are registered, you will receive an email with your login details. Check your spam folder if you do not find the email. This includes login details. You can also go directly to Office.com and login to the platform there.

What is P-20+ Education?

Most people know K-12 Education. P stands for Preschool. Then the "20" means 8 years after high school. The "+" means we go beyond the education system and step into business mentorship, too. So our P-20+ model helps people from preschool all the way into their business education needs once they have secured a career.

What is the 60 Day limit?

When you first sign up, you are automatically enrolled into a 60 day Welcome Course. Here, you do not have a teacher. Instead, you are expected to learn the software and navigate your way around our material to show that you have passion and aptitute in our environment. We give new registrants 60 days to make use of this software before we decide to accept them or turn them away. View this as our application process.

How do I pass the Application Process?

Currently, we are observing behavior in the Welcome Class to make sure applicants are not disrupting other student learning and to make sure applicants are using best-practices for their learning process. This means reading all the material, using all the tools we provide, and exhibiting excitement and participation in the Tao Cloud.

What if I want a Personal Mentor?

We offer a mentorship program if you desire a 1-on-1 personalized experience. You may find details on this program by visiting Tao Mentor. Here, you can book directly with a certified mentor. Our mentors can help boost your career and get your track on life with our innovative software and business connections. Each relationship is unique and we cater the program to your needs.
