Gathering Books for Children

12:00 am - 5:00 pm Montreal, Canada

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.”- Nelson Mandela. The development comes through education. Education helps to change the perspective and view of a person in a better way.

We gather books for children who cannot afford them. Donate your books and let's extend your helping hands towards the betterment and enlightenment of the future of these kids. Children of many underdeveloped countries are deprived of the basic rights they should get, they need help. They need proper education to break the barriers and bring out the light within them.

Event Content?

  1. Selfless participation in welfare works: charity for the needy.
  2. Providing campaigns regarding health and educational purpose.
  3. Providing the basic amenities to people from backward class.
  4. Helping victims of disasters; war, natural consequences, etc.
