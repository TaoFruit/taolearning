Corporate Partnerships

Tao Learning strongly believes in the power of partnerships and collaborative efforts and has rich history of working with the corporate sector.

We work closely with multi-national corporations, national companies and small to medium-sized businesses to identify, design and implement alliances that leverage the strengths of the corporate sector on behalf of the world’s education.

In turn, Tao Learning supports companies that aim to strengthen their commitment towards a positive contribution to the world’s communities and environment and provide support to achieve their CSR and business objectives.

Employee SMILE Program

We partner with corporations and organizations that have an invested interest in creating Self-Motivated Employees and SMILE Spaces in corporations. We create customized SMILE Networks, SMILE Spaces, and SMILE Summits specific to enterprise HR Initiatives. We help corporations understand the importance of P-20+ Education programs, implement education initiatives that create self-motivated staff, and provide ongoing training in STEAM literacy so employees can focus on their services and products instead of onboarding and training in general areas. With this approach, employers can reduce Hiring costs, Onboarding costs, and gain access to a broad array of candidates who have been trained in our learning methods for many years.

Our SMILE Program emphasizes Hive Mentality, which puts a large focus on teamwork, collaboration, and interactivity in international digial landscapes. Students who complete our literacy program excel at multiple sectors relevant to the ever-changing corporate environment where they have vast understanding of the importance of attitude, communication, and self-motivated techniques on their projects and tasks. For large corporations, we become the voice in the community to inspire community leaders to adopt STEAM literacy programs to intertwine with long-term community development in specialized skills needed by the local enterprises.

Junior Management Playground

We use a unique approach to education that allows you to use our platform to train low-skilled workers into the managers you wish them to become. We have many young students around the world who seek internship positions, yet have no experience whatsoever. Instead of weighing down your existing management, we encourage Enterprises to enroll their junior staff into our Management Playground program where they can have access to small teams and learn the skills needed to become adept leaders in your organization.

We cover literacy in multiple management techniques with the students of the management program with real-world experiences where they coach actual interns on actual projects. They are exposed to Agile, SCRUM, KanBan, and many other project management techniques while using enterprise software designed by Microsoft - such as Microsoft Project, Dynamics 365, PowerBI, Microsoft Flow, and Office 365. We encourage high prioritization of delegation, the use of artificial intelligence, and share skills that will increase their EQ while also increasing their literacy if they are to move up into a management position. While the overall program is dynamic, those enrolled may focus on areas their corporation sees as relevant where we will provide analytics and statistics back to the corporation on the skills learned and suggested continued coursework for leadership qualities.

About Tao Learning

Tao Learning is a community to advance education in a P-20+ Initiative, focusing on written, spoken, unspoken, and digital languages necessary to join the workforce and develop societies across the globe.

We have strong initiatives in USA, Pakistan, Africa, China, France, Costa Rica, and are continuing to expand our outreach in STEAM education. Our partners help provide more reasons, more funding, and more insight into the on-going need for enlightenment and awareness in literacy worldwide.
