Urgent Cause

#School for Islamabad's Students

In a place like Islamabad, where earning daily livelihood is tough for people, education is beyond their reach. Tao Learning raises fund for educational development in this area and also donates for school infrastructure.

Tao Learning’s main objective is to provide education to every child. Education cannot be provided from home to home. In order to impart correct education, schools are build for them. Islamabad’s main economic exchange depends on tin mining, we provide aids so that students can look forth towards a better standard of living that can be achieved by education.

The Program We Do?

  1. Raise funds to build school infrastructure.
  2. Arrange books for children.
  3. Provide sufficient aid to continue with education.
  4. Held educational camps to look after the working of these schools.
