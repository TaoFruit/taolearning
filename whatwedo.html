The SMILE Principle™

We encourage learning from preschool all the way to career growth. We have trained the same individuals for over 25 years and continue to encourage, inspire, and mentor curious minds of all ages.

We curate learning environments to be peaceful, flexible, and all-encompassing. Nobody wants to sit in small desks and get lectured. As lifelong learning, our members enjoy experiencing through internships, social hives, and so many more activities and atmospheres we create.

Our Projects

A few of our ongoing programs that have had great success.

PAKISTAN CODE UP

For years, we have created bootcamp style programs for children in 3rd world countries to get involved in STEAM, use high-tech computers, and get a chance to join the digital workforce.

SUPER LIBRARIANS

We offer young geniuses the chance to work 1-on-1 with a mentor to blossom their career. Whether they want to be their own boss or join an amazing company, we create the experience for them.

OHIO SMILE COTTAGE

A quiet and peaceful live-in education center where students guide their own learning as they explore areas in mental health, well-being, and hopefully find curiosity towards STEAM careers.
