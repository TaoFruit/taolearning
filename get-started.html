What is the Hive?

The Hive is where people from all over the world come together to learn, collaborate, chat, and socialize on projects together. It is the central hub of Tao Learning where you get to watch experts, participate in large projects that have an impact on the entire world, and meet like-minded students from all parts of the globe.

We have strong initiatives in USA, Pakistan, Africa, China, France, Costa Rica, and are continuing to expand our outreach in STEAM education.
